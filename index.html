Closest Pair of Points

We visualize a divide-and-conquer algorithm for finding a pair of points with the smallest distance between them. The dashed blue line indicates the boundary between the left and right sub-problems. The sub-problem under consideration at each time step is highlighted in green, and the strip examined during the merge step is in blue. The closest pair of points seen so far in the current subproblem is marked in red, and the final closest pair is connected with a green line.

Instructions: Click on the canvas to draw points. Press "return" to (re-)run the animation and "delete" to clear the canvas.
