Closest Pair of Points

We visualize a divide-and-conquer algorithm for finding the closest pair of points in the plane.

Instructions: Click on the canvas to draw points. Press "return" to (re-)run the animation.

The dashed lines (red) indicate the boundaries between the sub-problems, where their closest pair of points are connected by a grey line. The current subproblem being considered is highlighted in pink, and when we compare between results in left and right sub-problems, we highlight them in mustard. The strip considered in the merge step is highlighted in lavender.
